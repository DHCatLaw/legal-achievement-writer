## House voice
**ALWAYS**
- Name the firm consistently (no first-person "we/our firm").
- State result numbers on their own line.
- Break strategy into labeled, bolded points.
- Sign participating attorneys in the closing paragraph.
- Quote court language in the original, then gloss in plain language.

**NEVER**
- Melodrama ("dire situation", "cash flow devastated") — state facts plainly.
- Spotlight the opponent's mistakes instead of what the firm did.
- Client names without written consent.
- Docket / patent numbers unless explicitly requested.
- Sensational words / exclamation marks in titles.

## Confidentiality posture
- Default: **never name clients** unless written consent is on file.
- Default: **never disclose settlement terms** unless confirmed non-confidential and cleared by all parties.
