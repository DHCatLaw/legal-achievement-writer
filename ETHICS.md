# ETHICS.md — Professional Responsibility Notes

**English** · [中文](ETHICS.zh-CN.md)


This skill **produces attorney advertising**: public communications about a lawyer's services
and past results. Whether and how you may publish a case result is governed by the rules of
**every** jurisdiction in which you are admitted and in which you advertise. This document
explains the checks the skill enforces. **It is not legal advice and is not a substitute for the
current rules of your own bar.** Verify against the latest official text before relying on it.

> **For dual-qualified lawyers (e.g., China & U.S.):** your communication must satisfy **all**
> applicable regimes simultaneously. Where they differ, follow the stricter rule.

---

## United States

The skill's gate is built around the American Bar Association Model Rules; **your state's rules
control and frequently differ** (e.g., California has its own Rules of Professional Conduct).

- **Rule 1.6 — Confidentiality of Information.** A lawyer shall not reveal information relating
  to the representation absent informed consent or an exception. This is broader than "secrets":
  it covers most information about the matter. → *The skill defaults to no client name, and
  blocks disclosure of settlement terms and non-public facts.*
- **Rule 7.1 — Communications Concerning a Lawyer's Services.** A lawyer shall not make a false
  or misleading communication. A truthful statement is still misleading if it omits a fact needed
  to keep it from misleading, or creates an **unjustified expectation** about results. → *The
  skill blocks guarantees and "past results predict future results" implications, and many
  states require a disclaimer when advertising results.*
- **Rules 7.2 / 7.3** — advertising and solicitation constraints (varies by state).
- **No disparagement / unfair comparison** — unsubstantiated comparative claims can be misleading
  under 7.1. → *The skill bars elevating the firm by attacking opposing counsel or other firms.*

## People's Republic of China （中国）

- **《中华人民共和国律师法》(PRC Lawyers Law)** — establishes the lawyer's **duty of
  confidentiality** toward state secrets, commercial secrets, and client privacy.
  → *Mirrors the confidentiality gate above.*
- **All-China Lawyers Association — Rules on Lawyer Business Promotion**
  (《中华全国律师协会律师业务推广行为规则（试行）》; 律发通〔2018〕5号; in force). **Art. 10** bars,
  in business promotion: (1) false, misleading, or exaggerated promotion; (3) implying a special
  relationship with judicial/administrative organs; (4) disparaging other firms/lawyers or
  comparative advertising against them; (5) **promising case outcomes (承诺办案结果)**; (6) touting
  win rates / award amounts / matter values that may create **unreasonable public expectations**;
  (9) **publishing client information without the client's permission**. **Art. 8** — any honor
  cited must state its grantor and date. **Arts. 6–7** — promotional material must display the
  lawyer's name, bar license number, and firm name. **Art. 13** — these rules also apply when
  promoting through third-party platforms or media.
  → *Maps directly to the skill's no-guarantee, no-misleading, no-disparagement, fact-verification,
  and client-consent checks.*
- Advertising one's **past results (执业业绩)** is permitted content — but only if truthful and
  within the limits above.

> Citations verified against current primary-law text. **Your local bar association (地方律协) may
> impose additional or stricter requirements — confirm them before publishing.**

## The gate, mapped to the rules

| Skill check | U.S. basis | PRC basis |
|---|---|---|
| No client name without written consent | Rule 1.6 | 律师法 保密义务 |
| No settlement terms if confidential | Rule 1.6 + contract | 保密义务 + 约定保密 |
| Scrub privileged / non-public facts | Rule 1.6 | 保密义务 |
| Verify every fact vs. court record | Rule 7.1 | 禁止虚假/误导宣传 |
| No guarantee / no future-results implication | Rule 7.1 | 禁止胜诉等不当承诺 |
| No disparagement / unfair comparison | Rule 7.1 | 禁止诋毁同行 |

## Mandatory disclaimer (insert into every published article)

Use your jurisdiction's required language. Conservative bilingual default:

> **EN —** This describes an actual matter and is provided for informational purposes only.
> Results depend on the specific facts and law of each matter and vary accordingly. Prior
> results do not guarantee or predict a similar outcome in any future matter. This is not legal
> advice and does not create an attorney–client relationship.

> **中文 —** 本文所载案件结果均为真实发生之具体案例，仅供参考。案件结果受多种因素影响，因人因案
> 而异，过往业绩不代表未来结果，不构成对类似案件结果之任何保证或承诺，亦不构成法律意见或律师与
> 委托人关系之建立。

## A note on publishing this tool

Publishing a drafting tool is not itself advertising. But as a lawyer, ensure the tool, and any
example output you ship with it, does not contain client-identifying or confidential information.
The example in `examples/` is **entirely fictional** for this reason.
